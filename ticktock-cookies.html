Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better gaming experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our brain teaser games.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your game preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you interact with our puzzles and brain teasers
  • Improve our game performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and challenges
  • Ensure the security and functionality of our platform

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They include cookies for security, session management, and basic functionality.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our brain teaser games by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Preference Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features for your puzzle-solving experience.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our brain teaser games and your overall gaming experience.
