Privacy Policy

Introduction

At TickTock Challenge, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our brain teaser games and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as:

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or play our brain teaser games, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our brain teaser games
  • Personalize your gaming experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights
